# 🍽️ Yelp Business Analysis Project

## 📌 Introduction

This project focuses on analyzing Yelp business listing data to uncover insights related to customer engagement, business performance, category trends, geographic distribution, and customer satisfaction.

The project follows a complete data analytics workflow using **Python**, **MySQL**, and **Power BI** to transform raw business data into actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer engagement through reviews and ratings.
* Identify top-performing businesses and categories.
* Discover geographic business concentration across cities and states.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction trends.
* Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
* Extract business insights using SQL.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## 📊 Key Insights

### Top Reviewed Businesses

* Buffalo Wild Wings generated the highest review volume.
* Panera Bread and Olive Garden also showed strong customer engagement.

### Category Insights

* Plumbing contains the highest number of businesses.
* Delivery receives the highest average ratings.
* Delivery generates the highest review volume.
* Delivery provides the best balance of customer satisfaction and engagement.

### Geographic Insights

* Los Angeles has the highest concentration of businesses.
* California contains the highest number of businesses.
* Los Angeles contributes the largest share of customer reviews.
* New York and San Francisco are also highly active markets.

### Customer Satisfaction Insights

* Excellent-rated businesses generate the highest review volume.
* Approximately 29% of businesses maintain ratings above 4.0.
* Higher ratings generally lead to increased customer engagement.

### Hidden Opportunities

* Several businesses have perfect ratings but very few reviews.
* These businesses can grow through improved visibility and marketing efforts.
